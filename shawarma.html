Shawarma

Description

This delicacy, is just uh. I cant even fathom this shit bruh. It tastes soo delightful its beyond belief. The chicken with the prime white garlic sauce just melting in your mouth along with the chicken.. MMM TASTYYY

Shit you need to get

  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 large skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 ½ teaspoons garam masala
  • 4 pita bread rounds
  • 4 dill pickle spears

Shit you gotta do

  1. Stir together the garlic and mayonnaise in a small bowl; set aside. Heat the butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the chicken in the hot butter until white on the outside. Sprinkle with garam masala, and continue cooking until lightly browned on the outside and no longer pink in the center, about 4 minutes.
  2. Spread the pita rounds with the garlic mayonnaise. Divide the chicken among the pitas, and place a pickle spear into each. Fold and serve.
